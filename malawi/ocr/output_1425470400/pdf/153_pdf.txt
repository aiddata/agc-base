Co-ﬁnancing Agreement between Irish Aid and the Catholic Commission for Justice and

Peace (CCJP).

As part of lreland's Bilateral Aid Programme ("Irish Aid"), it has been agreed to provide E 226,678 in
ﬁnancial assistance to the Action for Better Service Delivery Project as set out in the project support:
document submitted to Irish Aid (attached). This contribution will support the work of the Catholic

Commission for Justice and Peace as per the project proposal and support documentation attached.

The substantive interface (including ﬁnancial) is between the Embassy of Ireland, Lilongwe and the
Catholic Commission for Justice and Peace. By this agreement the Catholic Commission for Justice
and Peace, shall be the executing agency. All documentation and notification regarding the Action for
Better Service Delivery Project should be sent to the Embassy oflreland, Arwa House, Lilongwe 3.

The following terms and conditions governing the making of the payment prevails:

l.

The Catholic Commission for Justice and Peace will ensure that the Action for Better
Service Delivery Project is carried out in accordance with the terms of the Project
Document provided (a copy of which is annexed hereto). The Catholic Commission
for Justice and Peace shall use the contribution solely for the purposes of the Project
and will apply it in the manner set out in the Project Document. The Catholic
Commission for Justice and Peace should also ensure that Irish Aid support is
appropriately acknowledged in all reports, studies etc. emanating from the Action for

Better Service Delivery Project.

Payments amounting to € 226,678 will be provided to the Catholic Commission for
Justice and Peace by Irish Aid as funding for the period 23” November 2009 — 30th
November 201] towards the costs incurred in relation to Action for Better Service
Delivery Project and the Catholic Commission for Justice and Peace agrees that such
monies will be applied in accordance with the Action for Better Service Delivery
Project, Project Document (attached).
