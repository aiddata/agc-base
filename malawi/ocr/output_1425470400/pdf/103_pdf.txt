Co—Financing Agreement between Irish Aid and International Crops
Research Institute for the Semi-Arid Tropics (ICRISAT)

As part of Ireland's Bilateral Aid Programme ("Irish Aid"), it has been agreed to provide
€450,000 in ﬁnancial assistance to the International Crops Research Institute for the Semi-Arid
Tropics (ICRISAT), and as set out in the enclosed programme document.

The substantive interface (including ﬁnancial) is between Irish Embassy, Lilongwe and
ICRISAT. By this agreement, the International Crops Research Institute for the Semi-Arid
Tropics, herein referred to as ICRISAT, shall be the executing agency. All documentation and
notiﬁcations regarding the Malawi Seed Industry Development should be sent to the
Embassy of Ireland, Private Bag B490, Arwa House, Lilongwe 3.

The following terms and conditions governing the making of the payments prevails:

l. ICRISAT will ensure that the Malawi Seed Industry Development is carried out in
accordance with the terms of the Project Support Document, provided (a copy of which
is annexed hereto). ICRISAT shall use the contribution solely for the purposes of the
Malawi Seed Industry Development, and will apply it in the manner set out in the
Project Support Document. ICRISAT should also ensure that Irish Aid support is
appropriately acknowledged in all reports, studies etc. emanating from the Malawi
Seed Industry Development.

2. (i) Payments amounting to €450,000 (the Aid Amount) will be provided to ICRISAT, by
Irish Aid as funding for the period 2009/2010 towards the costs incurred in relation to
the Malawi Seed Industry Development and ICRISAT agrees that such monies will
be applied in accordance with the Malawi Seed Industry Development, Project
Support Document (attached).

Accordingly, payment of the approved grant of €450,000 will be transferred to
ICRISAT in October, 2009.
